Wood-Fired Pizza Dough

Based On "I Slept In But I Want Pizza Tonight" Dough Recipe by Ken Forkish

  1. 350 g. Purified Water- 100F
  2. 10 g. Fine Sea Salt
  3. 1/8 t. Instant Dried Yeast
  4. 500 g. 00 Flour

Add salt to water. Swirl until dissolved.

Add yeast to water. Swirl gurl swirl.

Add flour to water mixture. Mix 30 seconds. Let rest 20 minutes.

Mix 30 seconds. Let rest 1.5 hours.

Divide and shape into three balls. Dust a work surface and work + shape into medium tight balls.

Place the balls onto a lightly-floured baking sheet. Sprinkle with flour and cover with plastic wrap. Let rest 4-6 hours.

READY FOR PIZZA BITCHES
